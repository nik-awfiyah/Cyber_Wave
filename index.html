About Us

Empowering Future Minds
with Technology

CyberWave Academy is a modern education center that blends academic excellence with essential digital skills to prepare students for the future. We offer tuition classes for both primary and secondary school students, covering core academic subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English and Malay. Beyond academics, we equip students with 21st-century skills including coding, graphic design, robotics and digital literacy. Our learning approach is interactive, engaging and tailored to nurture each student's full potential. At CyberWave Academy, we believe that a strong combination of academic knowledge an d tech skills will shape confident and future-ready individuals JOIN US AND RIDE THE WAVE OF THE FUTURE.
